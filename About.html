Why Signix exists

A reference, not just a feed.

We are building the first structured, searchable home for Kenyan Sign Language, documented by the people who sign it.

01
Documentation first

Every sign gets a handshape breakdown, movement path and real context, not just a clip to copy.

02
Built by signers

Creators who are fluent teach. Nothing is machine translated or AI generated without review.

03
One language at a time

KSL launches in full depth before the next language begins, with community consent.

Community Standard v1.0

Creator Guidelines

Signix is for real signers, not machine translation. Every upload teaches someone.

1. Accuracy First

Only upload signs you are fluent in. No guessing. If you are unsure of KSL/ASL/BSL variation, state the region in description. No AI-generated hands.

2. Respect Deaf Culture

No mocking, no sexualized signs, no making content about Deafness as a joke. Signix is built with Deaf reviewers.

3. Quality

Film horizontal, good lighting, plain background, hands and face visible. Max 100MB. No watermarks, no TikTok logos.

4. Consent

Everyone in the video must consent. No minors without guardian consent on camera. You own your video but you give Signix license to display it.

What gets removed
  • Wrong signs presented as correct, dangerous medical/legal signs by non-fluent creators
  • Hate, harassment in comments or signs
  • Nudity, sexual content, or signing in underwear/bathroom
  • Spam, self-promo unrelated to sign language, re-uploads of others' content
  • 3 strikes = account suspended

Reviews are done by fluent KSL signers. Appeals: support@signix.co.ke
